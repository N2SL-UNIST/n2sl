Minseok Kim, Daeun Lee, Yeon Ahn, Yunseo Jeong, and professor Young-bin Im attended [SIGCOMM 2025](https://conferences.sigcomm.org/sigcomm/2025/), the world’s premier academic conference in the field of networking, held in Coimbra, Portugal.

During the conference, our members had the opportunity to observe presentations of cutting-edge research by world-renowned scholars and experience firsthand the latest trends in next-generation network technologies. It was a highly productive time, allowing us to gain deep insights into global research directions, particularly in our lab’s core focus areas. Building on the inspiration gained from the field, we will continue to strive for more innovative research achievements.
